Managing Last Minute Cancellation of Manufacturing Order

Many a times, there are some last minute changes in plan; like you do not require the product anymore, or the quality is not suitable, etc. However, Odoo by default does not allow you to cancel the manufacturing Orders.

Not able to cancel the Manufacturing Order?
Here is the solution, Odoo Cancel Manufacturing Orders facilitates you to cancel the MO in Odoo which is even in a done state. The module helps you to make a streamlined process of canceling Manufacturing Orders by keeping in mind that there can be many reasons for last minute cancellation.

Mention the Reasons for Cancelling Manufacturing Order

Cancelling Manufacturing orders without mentioning reasons can create mess in future.
So, you have a chance to streamline the process; as you can create some preset reasons for cancelling MO in Odoo.

At the time of cancelling manufacturing order in Odoo, you can put up the preset reason along with the description.
This lets you have clear communication with the concerned person for the step taken.

Notify the manufacturer for Cancelled Manufacturing Order

When you cancel the manufacturing orders in bulk, it becomes tedious to inform each concerned person manually. The process of manually contacting people is not just time taking but requires a lot of efforts.

Odoo Cancel Manufacturing Orders has a solution for your problem; whenever you cancel the order, the concerned person will be notified. Hence, all types of confusions can be avoided.
